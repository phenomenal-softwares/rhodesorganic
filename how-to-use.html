How To Use Our Natural & Organic Beauty Cosmetics

Our products are safe and very easy to use, and the effects are noticeable just after very few days of application.

Small-area application
Apply only to small areas in a timely or time-limited manner.
Avoid eye contact
The products are strictly for external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. In case of irritation, discontinue use.
Restricted usage on children
Use prohibited on children under 6 years. Keep all products out of reach of children.
